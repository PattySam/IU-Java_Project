Club History

The Basketball Club was founded in 1990 with the goal of promoting and nurturing a passion for basketball. Over the years, the club has achieved numerous victories and milestones, contributing to the growth of the sport and creating a strong community of players, fans, and supporters.

Our journey has been marked by dedication, teamwork, and a commitment to excellence both on and off the court. From local tournaments to national championships, we have strived to be a beacon of sportsmanship and integrity.
